Priva

Meta's privacy policy, summarized:

Facebook collects information about you when you create an account, use their services, or interact with other users or businesses on the platform. This information includes your name, email address, phone number, location, and IP address, as well as the content and interactions you have on the platform. Facebook uses this information to provide and improve their services, including personalizing content and ads, and to detect and prevent fraud and spam. They may share your information with third-party partners, including advertisers and developers who create apps that integrate with Facebook. Facebook also uses your information for research and analytics purposes, and may share aggregated data with third parties. You have control over the information you share on Facebook, including your privacy settings and the ability to delete your account or data at any time. Facebook complies with applicable laws and regulations regarding data privacy, and takes measures to protect your information, such as encryption and access controls.
